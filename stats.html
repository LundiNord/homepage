Data Sources

Air Quality

The air quality data is provided by Landesanstalt für Umwelt Baden-Württemberg.

PM10 (inhalable): german limit: 40 µg/m³; WHO limit: 15 µg/m³

PM2,5 (respirable): german limit 25 µg/m³; WHO limit: 5 µg/m³

Weather

Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo.com.

Zippenfenig, P. (2023). Open-Meteo.com Weather API [Computer software]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/ZENODO.7970649

CO2 Levels

CO2 data is provided by NOAA.

Lan, X., Tans, P. and K.W. Thoning: Trends in globally-averaged CO2 determined from NOAA Global Monitoring Laboratory measurements. Version Friday, 14-Mar-2025 11:33:44 MDT https://doi.org/10.15138/9N0H-ZH07

German Yearly Average Temperature

The German yearly average temperature data is provided by DWD.

DWD Climate Data Center (CDC): Jährliche Gebietsmittel der Lufttemperatur (Jahresmittel) in °C (2 m Höhe), Version v19.3, abgerufen am 18.3.2024.

Inflation
