Privacy Policy

--------------------------------------------------

This privacy policy will explain how I and any necessary third-party services use the personal data collected from you when you use my website.

What data is collectd?

This website uses analytics to collect the following data:

time of access, what page you read, your operating system, your browser, the referer, user input events like button presses or inputs, from what country you're accessing this site, and some website diagnostics, like how long my homepage takes to load for you, or how long you are visiting.

This data is anonymized and no personal information is collected. No cookies are used.

This data is only accessible for six months by me.


This website is hosted by GitHub Pages, so their Privacy Policy applies.

The maps can use map tiles by ArcGis. Their Privacy Policy applies.

The maps can use map tiles by MapTiler. Their Privacy Policy applies.

Location and Routing search is provided by Mapbox. Your search queries are sent by your browser to Mapbox. Their Privacy Policy applies.

The air quality data is from Landesanstalt für Umwelt Baden-Württemberg. A request is sent by your browser to their server.

Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo. Their Privacy Policy applies.

Inflation dara is from the Federal Statistical Office of Germany. A request is sent by your browser to their server.

Privacy policies of other websites

My website contains links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to this website. If you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to the privacy policy
