This privacy policy will explain how I and any necessary third-party services use the personal data collected from you when you use my website.

What data is collectd?

This website used umami for analytics. Umami collects the following data:

time of access, what page you read, your operating system, your browser, the referer, from what country you're accessing this site and some website diagnostics, like how long my homepage takes to load for you, or how long you are visiting.

This data is anonymized and no personal information is collected. No cookies are used.

This data is only accessible for six months by Umami and me.
