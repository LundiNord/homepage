North Sweden 2024

Quick Facts:

  • Date: 22. Sep - 30. Sep
  • Distance: 139,74km
  • Weather: cold, lots of snow
  • Start: Nikkaluokta
  • End: Abisko
  • Arrival and departure: via sleeping Train

Day 0: The Start

After a long train ride from Germany to Kiruna, we took a bus to Nikkaluokta. We arrived around noon and hiked ~5km until we found a suitable camping spot.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 1: Good Weather

We started the adventure with beautiful weather.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 2: Glacier Adventures

Easy day because we got distracted by a beautiful glacier and just had to explore it. At noon, we met some nice people from the nearby research station. They were just about to leave for the winter. The last humans we saw for the next few days.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 3: Snowstorm

We woke up and everything was white. We walked what would with good weather be a route with beautiful views. But we saw mostly white.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

The snow made walking difficult, especially in the big boulder fields that seemed to never end.

Day 4: Escape from the mountains

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

It continued to snow the whole night, and the next morning it was already quite deep. We decided to leave the mountains and head for the valley.

As we were leaving the mountains, the weather cleared up, and we found a beautiful camping spot. We were rewarded with some northern lights later that night.

Day 5: The Moose Valley

As we walked through the valley, we saw loads of moose tracks in the snow. Sadly, we didn't see any living moose. But we met a hunter that just shot and gutted one.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 6: The upper end of the Valley

It started to thaw, but the weather was good, and we had a beautiful view of the valley as we ascended it. A truly magnificent valley!

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 7: Civilisation?

The weather continued to be good. We come across the popular Kungsleden hiking trail which has more the dimension of a hiking street. But nevertheless, no humans are in sight. For the rest of the hike, we followed the Kungsleden trail.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 8: Reindeer

We saw a reindeer herd. We also saw a lot of reindeer tracks.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 9: Side Adventure

As we (because of the bad weather) still had one day left, we decided to hike up a mountain. In theory, we had great views over Abisko National Park.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX

Day 10: The End

Back to civilization.

Distance: km Duration: Pace: GPX
